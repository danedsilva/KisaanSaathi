Who Is Eligible
✓All landholding farmer families with cultivable land — any size
✓Family defined as husband, wife, and minor children under 18
✓Land must be in farmer's name as of February 1, 2019 (cut-off)
✓Age minimum 18 years — no upper age limit
✓Aadhaar-linked bank account mandatory for DBT transfer
✓e-KYC completion is mandatory for all registered beneficiaries
🚫
Who Is NOT Eligible
✗Income tax payers (last assessment year)
✗Pensioners receiving ≥₹10,000/month
✗Current/former MPs, MLAs, Ministers
✗Constitutional post holders
✗Central/State Govt. employees (except Class IV/Group D)
✗Doctors, Engineers, Lawyers, CAs (registered professionals)
✗Institutional landholders
✗PSU/Autonomous body employees
📋
Documents Required
✓Aadhaar Card — mandatory for identity & e-KYC. Must be linked to mobile number for OTP
✓Land Records — 7/12 Utara, RTC, or Khasra. Must show cultivable land in farmer's name
✓Bank Passbook — Account must be Aadhaar-seeded. DBT payment sent here directly
✓Mobile Number — linked to Aadhaar for OTP-based e-KYC verification
✓Caste Certificate — for SC/ST farmers only, if applicable
✓IFSC Code — bank's branch code needed for correct DBT routing
🗺️
Registration Flow
Visit PM-KISAN Portal
Go to pmkisan.gov.in → Farmers Corner → New Farmer Registration. Select Rural or Urban Farmer.
Aadhaar Verification
Enter 12-digit Aadhaar number + mobile OTP. System checks if you're already registered.
Fill Registration Form
Enter name, address, bank details (account number + IFSC), and land holding information.
e-KYC Mandatory
Complete OTP-based e-KYC on the portal or biometric e-KYC at nearest Common Service Centre (CSC).
State Verification
State/district officials verify land records. This step may take 1–4 weeks.
DBT Payment Starts
Once approved, ₹2,000 credited directly to your Aadhaar-linked bank account every 4 months.
⚠️ e-KYC is MANDATORY — Don't miss your installment

The government has made Aadhaar-based e-KYC compulsory for all PM-KISAN registered farmers. Without it, installments will not be credited — even for previously eligible farmers.

Option 1 — Online (OTP)
1. Go to pmkisan.gov.in
2. Click "eKYC" in Farmers Corner
3. Enter Aadhaar + mobile OTP
4. Done in 2 minutes
Option 2 — Biometric (CSC)
1. Visit nearest Common Service Centre
2. Bring Aadhaar card
3. Fingerprint verification
4. Also: PM-KISAN Mobile App (Face Auth)
